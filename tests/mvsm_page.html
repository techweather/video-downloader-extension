Paper Pro Move

Campaign ~ reMarkable

Be There

We teamed up with the fine folk at reMarkable to introduce their innovative new product — the Paper Pro Move. A pocket-sized tablet fusing analog warmth with digital super-powers to keep human touch and true connection alive.

Captured on 16mm & 35mm film and paired with tactile 2D line animation, the films follow three different professionals throughout their working life, showcasing the importance of human connection and the innate power of a digital notepad in the modern age.
